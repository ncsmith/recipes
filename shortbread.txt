This recipe is for shortbread. The file was created by Nate Smith the recipe is from Laura Mondt

Ingredients
 2 sticks butter
.5 C sugar
2.333 C Flour

Prep
cream butter and sugar till fluffy. Slowly blend flour. Add sprinkles if desired. Preheat oven 325 F

Pickup
Grease 9x13 pan. Bake 35-40 minutes. Cut immediately
